# Personas

The personas below are based only on visible roles and workflows from public pages.
Directly named roles are marked with high confidence.
Inferred permissions or role boundaries are labeled where the public site does not spell them out.

## Jogador / Cliente / Usuário
- Role: person looking for a court and making a booking
- Responsibilities: browse available courts, choose a date and time, submit a booking, pay, and optionally organize the match
- Expected goals: book a court quickly, avoid calling the facility, see availability in real time, and coordinate game logistics
- Typical workflows: open the app without an account, search by location, choose a slot, pay by PIX or card, or request a local payment that needs manager approval, invite friends, and sort teams
- Permissions inferred: can browse the app without an account; can create bookings; can organize a match through the app
- Confidence: High

## Gestor(a) da quadra / Responsável
- Role: facility owner or manager responsible for the court operation
- Responsibilities: register the facility, configure it in App Gestor, receive booking requests, manage schedules, activate payments, and control finance
- Expected goals: reduce manual work, avoid cancellations hurting revenue, and centralize the operation
- Typical workflows: sign up on the site, download the manager app, create or credenciar a digital account, activate PIX/card receipts, manage agenda and cash flow, and configure public visibility in App Agendei
- Permissions inferred: can manage one or many courts, approve local-payment bookings, set cancellation rules, create day-use offers, and share access with staff or partners
- Confidence: High

## Funcionário / Colaborador
- Role: staff member who participates in daily court management
- Responsibilities: help manage schedules and operational tasks under shared access
- Expected goals: perform routine management without needing the full owner account
- Typical workflows: access shared management profiles and work on bookings or schedules assigned by the facility
- Permissions inferred: the public site says employees can access the app for schedule management, but exact permissions are not publicly detailed
- Confidence: Medium

## Sócio / Co-gestor
- Role: business partner who shares responsibility for the facility
- Responsibilities: collaborate in administration and oversight
- Expected goals: manage the facility together with the owner or other managers
- Typical workflows: use shared access profiles in the manager app
- Permissions inferred: shared-management access can be created for partners; the exact permission model is not publicly detailed
- Confidence: High
