# Product Overview

## Product Purpose
Agendei Quadras is a sports court booking and management platform.
The public site positions it as a system that connects players to courts and gives facilities tools to manage schedules, payments, cancellations, day use, and shared operations.

## Target Audience
- Players who want to find and book courts
- Facility managers or owners who run one or more courts
- Staff and business partners who share operational access
- Prospective court operators evaluating the platform

## High-Level Positioning
The product is presented as an end-to-end operating surface for sports facilities.
The player-facing app handles discovery and booking, while the manager-facing app handles agenda control, monetization, and administration.
The public site also markets payment infrastructure through Agendei Pay.

## Main Operational Goals
- Help players find courts quickly by location and availability
- Let facilities receive and manage bookings without paper or spreadsheets
- Support online payment by PIX or card
- Reduce revenue loss from late cancellations
- Allow day use, discounts, tournaments, lessons, and shared access to be managed in one place

## Major Business Areas
- Court discovery and booking
- Booking confirmation and payment
- Cancellation control
- Daily access sales
- Financial control and cash flow
- Shared administration and staff access
- Promotions, coupons, lessons, and events
- Support and onboarding for facility operators

## Visible Applications
- `App Agendei Quadras` - player-facing app for finding and booking courts
- `App Gestor Agendei` - manager-facing app for facility operations
- `Agendei Pay` - financial and payout layer tied to online bookings
- Public website - onboarding, plans, FAQ, and product explanation

## Product Positioning Notes
- The public app is advertised as available in Google Play and the App Store
- The player flow is presented as simple, fast, and suitable for users who do not want to call the facility
- The manager flow is presented as a replacement for paper, spreadsheets, and manual scheduling
