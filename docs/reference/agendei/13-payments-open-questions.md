# Payments Open Questions

## Purpose
This file tracks what could not be confirmed from public sources.
It is limited to observable gaps in the public billing and payments material.

## Payment Method Questions
- Does PIX use QR code, copy-and-paste, or both in the app?
- Is card payment limited to credit card, or is debit or installment support also available?
- What exact payment options exist for local payment at the facility?
- Can a player choose more than one payment method for the same booking?

## Confirmation and Status Questions
- What exact booking statuses exist for paid, pending, accepted, cancelled, refunded, and no-show flows?
- Does local payment create a provisional booking state before manager approval?
- Can the manager override the automatic confirmation rule for PIX or card bookings?
- Is there any pending state before PIX or card are treated as confirmed?

## Settlement and Repass Questions
- Does the digital account belong to the facility, a legal representative, or both?
- Which institution or provider holds the digital account?
- Can funds be withdrawn instantly at any time, or only after settlement?
- Do weekend rules apply only to PIX, or also to card settlement?
- Can transfers go to any bank, or only to registered accounts?

## Cancellation and Refund Questions
- Who can initiate cancellation?
- Can the manager override the penalty percentage or the tolerance window?
- Does rescheduling avoid the cancellation penalty?
- Are no-shows handled differently from cancellations?
- Are refunds automatic for every payment method?
- How are disputes or chargebacks handled?

## Financial Management Questions
- What reports are visible in the financial management area?
- Can managers reconcile bookings with payouts and cash movements?
- Does the cash flow view show daily, weekly, or monthly summaries?
- Are manual financial entries supported?

## Coupons and Discounts Questions
- Who creates coupons and where in the product is that done?
- Can coupons be limited by date, court, player, or number of uses?
- Can coupons stack with other discounts?
- Are coupons usable for day use, only hourly bookings, or both?

## Future Billing Feature Questions
- Is `Gerador de Cobranças` currently available or only announced?
- What does the billing generator actually produce?
- How does material rental relate to checkout and billing?
- Does the announced AI feature affect billing or only operations?
