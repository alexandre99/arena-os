# CRM Open Questions

## Purpose
This file tracks what could not be confirmed from public sources.
It is limited to the CRM-related gaps visible in the public product material.

## Client Profile Questions
- What exact fields exist on the customer profile?
- Is login based on phone, email, CPF, social account, or another identity?
- Can a customer browse before creating an account on every surface, or only on App Agendei?
- Is the invitation identity the same as the booking identity?
- Is the client profile shared across facilities or scoped to a single facility?

## Customer History Questions
- What history screens exist for bookings, payments, cancellations, and day use?
- Is booking history visible to players, managers, or both?
- Does the app show past and upcoming bookings separately?
- Is payment history visible as a customer statement or ledger?
- Is chat retained by booking, by customer, or both?

## Communication Questions
- Is chat per booking or per customer?
- Do staff and partners see messages?
- Are notifications push, email, WhatsApp, or in-app only?
- Are booking confirmations and cancellation notices sent automatically?
- Is there a public support channel beyond the contact entry points on the website?

## Coupon and Engagement Questions
- Who creates coupons, and where in the product is that done?
- Can coupons be limited by date, court, player, or number of uses?
- Can coupons stack with other discounts?
- Are coupons usable for day use, only hourly bookings, or both?
- How do friend invitations and team sorting work internally?

## Retention Questions
- Does mensalista mean a recurring billing relationship, a fixed booking relationship, or both?
- Can a customer hold more than one fixed booking?
- Are lessons and tournaments tied to repeat-customer retention in practice?
- Does day use have repeat-customer history or only one-off sales?
- Are chat and coupons part of an explicit retention workflow, or only operational features?

## Permissions Questions
- Which customer data can managers see?
- Can staff and partners view or edit customer records?
- Are permissions scoped by facility, by court, or by feature?
- Can customer access be blocked or revoked without affecting active bookings?
- Can different roles see different parts of the same customer history?
