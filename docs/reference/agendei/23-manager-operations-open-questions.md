# Manager Operations Open Questions

## Purpose
This file tracks what could not be confirmed from public sources.
It is a focused list of follow-up questions, not a gap analysis or product recommendation.

## Facility Onboarding Questions
- What exact facility fields are required at signup?
- Is access to `App Gestor` immediate after registration or subject to review?
- Which documents are mandatory for the responsible person?
- Can CPF registration be used for all facilities or only specific cases?
- Are there public approval or rejection states for new facilities?

## Configuration Questions
- What exact court fields exist in the manager setup flow?
- How are opening hours and exception days configured?
- Can prices vary by court, day, or time slot?
- Can cancellation tolerance vary by court, booking type, or time band?
- Can coupons target a single court or a subset of bookings?
- How is day use edited after publication?

## Shared Management Questions
- Which permissions can staff receive beyond schedule access?
- Which permissions can partners or co-managers receive?
- Are access profiles predefined or custom?
- Can a manager revoke access and see prior activity?
- Does shared management include `Agendei Pay` and finance controls?

## Multi-Court Questions
- Are courts configured independently or inherited from the facility?
- Can each court have different availability and pricing rules?
- Can a single account manage multiple facilities, or only multiple courts in one facility?
- Is visibility controlled per court or only at the facility level?

## Plan and Packaging Questions
- Is billing monthly, annual, or both in practice?
- Does the free trial always last 15 days?
- Are there usage limits by court, user, or booking volume?
- Are transaction fees plan-based or flat across all tiers?
- Are announced features on a fixed release schedule?
- Are plan-specific permissions or entitlements visible to the manager?

## Operations Questions
- What does the manager dashboard actually surface for finance?
- Is chat used only for booking-related messages or for broader communication?
- How do lessons / escolinha work operationally?
- How do tournament registrations work operationally?
- Is `Central de Atendimento` human-assisted, self-service, or both?

## Evidence Gaps
| Missing information | Why it matters | Suggested future source |
|---|---|---|
| Exact onboarding form fields and required documents | Needed to describe signup friction and compliance accurately. | Authenticated walkthrough, public FAQ page, or demo call |

| Exact permission matrix for staff and partners | Needed to distinguish delegation from ownership. | Authenticated walkthrough or user interview |
